Roles and Responsibilities


Functions of the Students' Council


  1. Energise the Collge to a common identity and spirit.
  2. Engage with the cohort to understand and seek to fulfil their needs and aspirations.
  3. To serve the College.

Executive Committee (ExCo)


  1. Envisions what the college spirit lokks like in ASR and sets the direction for the current cohort to move the college towards that vision.
    • This includes an awareness of what is going on in the college and the activities students are involved in.
  2. Coordinates the work within the Council to achieve the direction. This includes, but is not limited to:
    • Monitoring the progeress of each work group and ensuring that the work group's objectives are aligned to the direction.
    • Planning and running CCA sessions.
  3. Coordinates the Council in keeping in touch with the needs and aspirations of the student population and engaging with the relevant stakeholders to advocate for their peers.
  4. Take care of the wellbeing of the Council members.
  5. Responsible for the development of the new intake of the Council.

         Given the above collective functions of the ExCo, the respective roles and responsibilities of each position are as follows:

  1. President
    • To ensure that the Council is aligned to the direction and works to achieve the direction.
    • To reach out to relevant stakeholders outside of Council to forge partnerships to achieve the direction.
    • To ensure that the work within Council is coherent and working in concert with each other to achieve the direction.
    • To ensure that Council members are meaningfully deployed and engaged and that their wellbeing is taken into account.
  2. Vice-Presidents
    • To support the President in ensuring that the Council is aligned to the direction and works to achieve the direction.
    • To support the President in reaching out to relevant stakeholders outside of Council to forge partnerships to achieve the direction.
    • To support the President in ensuring that the work within Council is coherent and working in concert with each other to achieve the direction.
    • To support the President in ensuring that Council members are meaningfully deployed and engaged and that their wellbeing is taken into account.
  3. Student Welfare Committee Head
    • To ensure that the Council is constantly able to understand the needs and aaspirations of the student population.
    • To ensure that the needs of the student population are constructively represented to the college.
    • To work with the House Captains in planning initiatives/activities that serve to build up College Spirit.
  4. Communications Committee Head
    • Directs the narrative of the college through platofrms inclduing but not limited to social media, noticeboards and the interactive television.
    • Coordinates the work of all social media platforms, including asr.jcians and asr.sc and the House Instagram pages.
    • Streamlines all external Communication of the Council.

House Captains


  • Work with the ExCo in planning initiatives/activities that serve to build up the House Spirit to achieve a strong College Spirit.
  • Work with the ExCo to constantly be aware of the needs and aspirations of the House population and to constructively repressent them to the College during Orientation.
  • Lead the College Ambassadors in supporting initiatives/activities that serve to build up House and College Spirit.
